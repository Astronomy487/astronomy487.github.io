This is an engine for writing online choose-your-own-adventure stories. Writing them really isn't hard.

Each page on an adventure can consist of heading(s), image(s), paragraph(s), and choice(s), in any order. You should first plan out your story, and what will be included on a page.

Each page begins by declaring its internal name after an @ sign. If you want to create a page called "morning", you would write @morning on its own line. In subsequent lines, you can create headers with #, images by url after a *, and paragraphs with >. To include a choice, you have to include the internal page name between two forward slashes, and put choice text afterwards. The example adventure seen on the home page looks like this:

@wake
#You woke up.
*https://media0.giphy.com/media/7zMvSDWl3RkDSpOjBC/giphy-downsized-large.gif
>Good morning
/zzz/Go back to sleep
/out of bed/Get out of bed

@zzz
#You went back to bed
*https://i.pinimg.com/originals/2c/bb/bd/2cbbbdff62e5faba5c761d7c48c1957e.gif
>zzzzzzzz you're sleeping
/wake/zzz...

@out of bed
#You died.
/wake/try again

Once you have this written, run it through a base64 encoder like base64encode.org. This step is just to obfuscate the adventure to readers, so that they aren't spoiled just by putting in your story.
