lipu pi nimi ale
mi pali e lipu nimi ni tan ni: mi ken ala lukin e lipu nimi kepeken nimi ale. lon lipu ni la mi wile alasa e nimi ale pi toki pona tawa lipu wan. mi wile e nimi pi kepeken lili e nimi musi nasa. mi kepeken e lipu mute tawa jo e nimi la pona a tawa mama ona. I made this wordlist because I couldn't find any dictionaries that had all of the words. With this page, I want to collect all Toki Pona words into one place, even barely used ones or joke ones. I'm using lots of resources to get the words, so thanks to all those creators.
ken la nimi li lon li weka. sona la kon nimi mute li weka. pali ni li pini ala. I am probably missing some words. I know that lots of words are missing definitions. This project is ongoing.
